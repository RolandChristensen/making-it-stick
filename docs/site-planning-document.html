Website Planning Document

Checklist for planning a website

Topic Web Developer Questions Answers (Give brief but thoughtful and realistic answers with the cooperation of all team members.)
Purpose of Website What is the purpose and goal of the website? I want a site that supplements the book "Make it Stick" used to enhance learning.

What is the company's mission statement? Evidence based learning tools for lifelong learning.
Target Audience Describe the target audience (age, gender, demographics) Students who want to increase their ability to learn new skills in a persistent and useful way. The focus is on lifelong learners, so age is not a primary concern. I want to make this as accessible as possible to people from all walks of life.

What information is most pertinent to the users? There should be detailed instructions about how to use the site to study for classes with the focus on creating durable and useful memories that will enable mastery of the subject matter.
Multi-platform Display Will you design for multiple platform display or only focus on a desktop or mobile design? I want to create a site for each platform (phone, tablet, desktop, ultra-wide), with intelligent breakpoints based on the content.
Wireframe How many pages will be included in the website? Growing number based on user input. Six pages to start with.

How will the pages be organized? Home, About, Contact, Flash Cards Instructions, Dynamic Subject Creation, Dynamic Flash Card Pages

What type of website structure is appropriate for the content? Webbed design: Each page should have a menu, that will take us Home, About, and Contact; on the menu there should also be a link to each subject added, with the pages underneath.
Linear design: Each Flash Card Page should have navigation that will take you to the previous or next page.
Algorithmic: Design algorithm to push you to study things you have not after a period of time, a pop up or other notification can alert you and will flow in a somewhat linear fashion to guide you through material that has not been studied for a while.
Graphics What graphics will you use on the website? Images of students studying and books.
Color What colors will you use within the site to enhance the purpose and brand? Snow on Light Grey (#C6C6C6)
Snow on Dark Grey (#706F6F)
Black on Snow (#FFFAFA)
Snow on Light Purple (#D2DAED)
Typography What font styles will you use within the website? Roboto

Accessibility How will the website accommodate people with disabilities? I want to try to run this site as if I were blind.
